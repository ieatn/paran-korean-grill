Our Story

Where Speedy
Meets Special

PARAN lives at the intersection of fast and thoughtful — a Korean corner spot in Fremont where bold grills, crispy mandoo, and ice-cold drinks come together without ceremony. New-generation Korean dining that respects the craft without taking itself too seriously.

Counter seating puts you face-to-face with the kitchen. The menu is tight on purpose. Every dish earns its place. Come hungry, leave happy, come back often.
